# Investigation Builder

Build investigations around evidence chains.

## Build Sequence

1. define the target claim or anomaly
2. identify independent evidence sources
3. normalize entities, dates, and identifiers
4. compare records for mismatches
5. record why each mismatch matters
6. stop short of conclusions the evidence cannot support

## Output

Provide:
- investigated claim
- evidence summary
- anomaly list
- confidence or uncertainty note
- recommended next verification step
