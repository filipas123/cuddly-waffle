# Meeting Intelligence Builder

Build meeting materials that help the user enter the room prepared.

## Build Sequence

1. define the meeting objective
2. identify stakeholders and context
3. identify likely tensions or objections
4. build agenda and talking points
5. define desired outcomes
6. prepare follow-up actions

## Output

Provide:
- one-paragraph brief
- agenda
- stakeholder notes
- risks or sensitivities
- recommended asks or decisions
- post-meeting follow-up list
